Features

nothing they don't

Smart Scheduling

Assign multiple family members to the same chore on the same day. Weekly or biweekly recurring tasks with one-tap setup.

⭐

Points & Gamification

Earn points for every chore. Build streaks, unlock achievements, and compete on the family leaderboard. Early bird bonuses before noon.

💰

Allowance Tracking

Link chore points directly to allowance. All-or-nothing mode means every task matters. Track payouts and balances.

📍

Family GPS Map

See where everyone is in real-time on a family map. Background tracking on mobile. Privacy controls built in.

Real-Time Sync
